Who We Are

Your Global Supply Partner

Jiangyi Food Co., Ltd and Bloom New Material Co., Ltd. form a comprehensive export group based in Shandong, China — home to the largest garlic production region in the country. We specialize in the export of agricultural products, packaging materials, and industrial supplies.

With years of experience serving markets across the Middle East, Africa, Southeast Asia, and beyond, we pride ourselves on reliable supply chains, strict quality control, and customer-centric service.
